# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

Do’s 

--> Always use strong passwords to keep your accounts safe. 

--> Think carefully before posting anything online. 

--> Try to share positive and useful content. 

--> Be respectful while interacting with others. 

--> Maintain a clean and professional profile. 

Don’ts 

--> Don’t share your personal or private information publicly. 

--> Avoid posting anything offensive or inappropriate. 

--> Don’t trust or spread fake news without checking it. 

--> Stay away from online arguments or negative discussions. 

--> Never click on suspicious links or unknown messages. 

 
## Conclusion
Social media can be a powerful tool when used responsibly. By following these do’s and don’ts, college students can maintain a safe, respectful, and professional online presence.
